December 8th

The wise men did not come the night Jesus was born as the shepherds did. They saw the star in the east and traveled far to see him. They arrived in Palestine from the east sometime later, perhaps even months later. When Herod sent the wise men to find Jesus, did they return to Herod? Where did they go?

Read Matthew 2:1-12

The first person who answered the question correctly will find a treat under the couch. Like the wise men, you have a choice to either share or keep your treat. Which will it be?
